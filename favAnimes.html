The best animes according to me :)

These are my top 3 animes of all time.

Kimi ni todoke

Well this is a romantic anime about two high school students. The reason that its my favorite anime is because how pure it is, unlike other rom coms that builds unrealistic standards.


Death Note

Death Note is a psychological thriller about a high school student who finds a supernatural notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it.


Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy anime about humanity's fight for survival against giant humanoid Titans.
